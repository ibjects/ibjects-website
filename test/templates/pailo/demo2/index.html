Is Pailo safe for kids?

Yes—Pailo is carefully designed with safety and privacy as our top priority. Perfect for curious kids aged 9–14 who love games, creativity, and learning.

What is Pailo?

Pailo is a avatar friend that helps you with your daily tasks and provides you with useful tips and ideas. To get started, simply download our app from Play or App store.

How is the data protected?

Your data is securely stored in our servers and no third party has access to any of the data. You can submit a request to delete all the data anytime.

Yes! You can chat, play, and customize your avatar totally free. You can buy different avatar items to customize avatar items and more interactions with the avatar.
