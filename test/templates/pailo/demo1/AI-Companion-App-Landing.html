Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AI Buddy.

AI Buddy learns through your interactions. As you chat and use the app, it observes patterns in your preferences, communication style, and needs. You can also directly customize settings to help it learn faster. All learning happens on-device or with encrypted data to protect your privacy.

Yes, we take privacy very seriously. Your conversations are encrypted, and you have complete control over what data is stored. We never sell your data to advertisers or third parties. You can delete your data at any time, and we offer transparent privacy controls within the app.

AI Buddy offers limited functionality offline. Basic conversations and previously cached information are available without an internet connection. However, for the full experience including up-to-date information and advanced features, an internet connection is recommended.

AI Buddy is available on iOS and Android smartphones and tablets. We also offer a web version for desktop users. Your account and preferences sync across all your devices, so you can switch seamlessly between them.

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings in the app, or through your app store account. After cancellation, you'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period, then automatically revert to the free plan.
