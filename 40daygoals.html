40Day Goals App

At 40DayGoals, we value your privacy and believe in keeping things simple. Here's a breakdown of our approach:

Confidentiality of Your Goals: Your goals are your own, and we respect that. We don't ask for personal details like your name or email. Your journey with 40DayGoals is solely about you and your achievements.

No Account, No Hassle: We've done away with the need for accounts. When you download the 40DayGoals app, you receive a unique ID. It's your personal key to unlock and access your data securely. Plus, you can add an extra layer of protection with a password.

Your Unique ID - Your Control: Your unique ID is your gateway to your goals. With a password, you have complete control over who accesses your data. It's all about empowering you while keeping your information safe.

We Keep it In-House: Rest assured, your data is yours and yours alone. We don't share it with any third parties. Your unique ID and goals are stored securely in the cloud, solely for the functionality of the app.

Why We Collect Goals Data: We collect your goals data against the unique ID you receive for one simple reason – to enhance your experience with the app. Your data is used for app functionality purposes, ensuring a seamless and personalized journey towards your goals.

That's it – your privacy, your goals, your control. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out. We're here to make your 40DayGoals experience as awesome and private as possible.
